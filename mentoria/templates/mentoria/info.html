C-Mentor Network

C-Accelerate Plus is a flagship initiative designed to empower students with innovation, entrepreneurship, and industry-ready skills.

C-Mentor Network app is a dedicated platform connecting students with experienced mentors to guide them through innovation, entrepreneurship, and career growth.

Become a Mentor Become a Mentee

About the Program

In this program, more experienced students (mentors) support other students (mentees) to improve academic performance and autonomy, while promoting personal and social development.

  1. The app is to be used by students registered on it by the academic services.
  2. Students register as mentors and/or mentees, specifying the subjects.
  3. Mentors select available mentees, creating mentor–mentee pairs.
  4. The mentor contacts the mentee to schedule a session.
  5. After each session, the mentor records the session in the application.
  6. Once recorded, the mentee confirms and provides feedback.
