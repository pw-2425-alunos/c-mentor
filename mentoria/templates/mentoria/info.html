What is C-Mentor?

C-Mentor Network is a university-driven initiative where experienced students (mentors) support their peers (mentees) to boost academic performance, autonomy, and personal growth.

How does it work?

  1. 1 Students are registered in the app by a C-Mentor Network university manager.
  2. 2 Students select mentor and/or mentee roles, specifying their mentorship subject(s).
  3. 3 Mentors select available mentees in their mentoring subjects, forming mentor–mentee pairs.
  4. 4 Mentors schedule mentoring sessions with mentees.
  5. 5 Mentors submit feedback and evaluation for each concluded session.
  6. 6 Mentees confirm the completed session and provide feedback.
  7. 7 Managers review and report sessions for students’ benefit.
  8. 8 Managers monitor application usage through a dashboard with multiple indicators.
