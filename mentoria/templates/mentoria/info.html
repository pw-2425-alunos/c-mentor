About the Program

With C-Mentor Network, more experienced students (mentors) support other students (mentees) to improve academic performance and autonomy, while promoting personal and social development.

Getting started

  1. The app is to be used by students registered on it by the C-Mentor Network responsible of their university.
  2. Students register as mentors and/or mentees, specifying the subjects.
  3. Mentors select available mentees, creating mentor–mentee pairs.
  4. The mentor contacts the mentee to schedule a session.
  5. After each session, the mentor records the session in the application.
  6. Once recorded, the mentee confirms and provides feedback.
